Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 20, 2026

Somnia Studio ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application RainMixer.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • We do not collect personal information unless you voluntarily provide it to us.
  • If you contact us for support, we may collect your email address and any information you choose to provide.

Usage Information

  • We may collect information about how you use the app, such as features accessed and time spent in the app.
  • This information is collected anonymously and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Device Information

  • We may collect information about your device, including device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
  • This information helps us optimize the app's performance and compatibility.

Advertising Information

Our app uses Google AdMob to serve advertisements. Through AdMob, certain information may be automatically collected, including:

  • Advertising ID — Your device's advertising identifier (e.g., Google Advertising ID or Apple IDFA), used to serve personalized ads.
  • Device Information — Device type, model, operating system, and version.
  • IP Address — Used to estimate general location (country or city level) for ad targeting.
  • Ad Interaction Data — Information about ads viewed, clicked, or interacted with within the app.
  • App Usage Data — How you interact with the app, including session duration and feature usage, to help deliver relevant ads.
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies — AdMob may use cookies, pixel tags, or similar technologies to collect data and serve ads.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our app
  • Improve user experience
  • Respond to customer support requests
  • Analyze app usage patterns to enhance functionality
  • Display advertisements through Google AdMob

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights or property
  • With your explicit consent

Third-Party Advertising — Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display ads in our app. AdMob is provided by Google LLC and may collect and process data as described above to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. Information shared with Google AdMob may include:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Ad interaction and performance data
  • App usage and crash diagnostics

Google may use this data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. You can learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use their services at Google Partner Sites.

Your Ad Choices

You can control how ads are personalized by:

  • Android: Go to Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalization" or "Delete advertising ID".
  • iOS: Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, and disable tracking for RainMixer. You can also enable "Limit Ad Tracking" under Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising.

Opting out of personalized ads does not remove ads from the app; it means the ads you see will be less relevant to your interests.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@somniastudio.app
